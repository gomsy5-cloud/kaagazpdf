FAQ

Questions? Hum hain na.

Are my documents really safe? +
Yes. KaagazPDF processes files using in-browser technology (WebAssembly + JavaScript), so your document is opened and edited on your own device. It is never uploaded to any server — you can even switch off your internet after the page loads and tools will still work. Additionally, the working session auto-clears every hour.
What does "5 free downloads per day" mean? +
You can process and download up to 5 files every day at no cost, supported by ads. The counter resets at midnight IST. Pro users (₹299/month) get unlimited downloads, no ads, and the Pro-only tools (Image Enhancer, Passport Photo Sheet & background change).
Do I need an account to use KaagazPDF? +
You can open any tool and process files without an account, but a quick free sign-up (just name + email) is needed to download your finished file. This is how we track your 5 free daily downloads fairly across devices. Pro (₹299/month) is linked to the same account, so unlimited works everywhere you log in. Your documents themselves are never uploaded or linked to your account.
How do I pay for Pro? +
Pay via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), debit/credit card, or net banking. It's a simple ₹299/month plan that you can cancel anytime — no lock-in, no hidden charges.
Do the tools work on mobile? +
Bilkul! KaagazPDF works on any modern browser — Android, iPhone, laptop or desktop. No app installation required.
What happens during the hourly auto-clear? +
Every 60 minutes, all files and working data in your session memory are wiped automatically. This is an extra safety layer on top of the fact that nothing was ever uploaded in the first place.
