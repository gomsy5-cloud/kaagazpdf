About KaagazPDF

India ka apna PDF toolkit — private by design

KaagazPDF is a free suite of 21 document and image tools built specifically for the way people in India handle paperwork — exam forms, passport applications, PAN and Aadhaar copies, scanned certificates and everyday PDFs.

Our promise: your files never leave your device

Most online PDF websites work by uploading your document to their servers, processing it there, and asking you to trust that they delete it afterwards. For files that contain your Aadhaar number, bank statements, mark sheets and photographs, that is a real privacy risk. KaagazPDF was built on the opposite principle: every tool runs inside your own browser using in-browser technology, so your file is opened, edited and saved entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can even disconnect from the internet after the page loads and the tools still work.

What you can do

KaagazPDF covers the full range of everyday document work: merge, split, compress, rotate and organise PDFs; convert between PDF and images; add page numbers, watermarks and signatures; unlock restricted PDFs; convert PDF to Word; and run OCR to read text from scans in English, Hindi and Bengali. It also includes India-specific tools you won't find elsewhere — a document redactor to permanently hide sensitive numbers, a passport photo-sheet maker, and preset photo/signature resizers built to match exactly what government forms demand.

Free, fair and affordable

Every core tool is free to use, supported by ads, with a set number of downloads each day. For people who work with documents constantly — cyber cafés, coaching centres, busy professionals — the Pro plan removes ads, unlocks unlimited downloads and larger files, and adds a couple of premium tools, for a simple ₹299 per month that you can cancel anytime. We priced it for India, not against it.

Made in India

KaagazPDF is built and run in India, with an interface that speaks the way people here actually do — a little English, a little Hindi, and a lot of getting-it-done. Our goal is simple: make the most frustrating part of any application — the document step — take seconds instead of an hour, without ever compromising your privacy.
